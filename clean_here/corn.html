Corn! Glorious Corn!

A tongue-in-cheek humourous website about corn. My sister, Erin, has a soft spot for corn related humour.For her 36th birthday, I made her this. This was the first project I ever built on my own.

DISCLAIMER

There is one section of this project that is a bit NSFW. The "cornography" page has pictures of corn titled with captions that contain sexual innuendo.

Built With:

Django, AJAX, JavaScript, JQuery, CSS3, HTML5. AJAX is used to populate the corn stories on the home page with the most recent story appearing first.
